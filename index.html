Some of my works!!

Hello World - a small step towards humanity

Hello World is an Android application which helps you to trace out needy person and donate that needy person. This application has an attractive UI and requires Google Sign-In. It uses Firebase to store the data.
Whenever you spot a needy person you just need to take your phone out and it will show you two options SPOTTED A NEEDY and DONATE A NEEDY. When you click on SPOTTED A NEEDY you are popped up with a camera and you need to click the picture of him/her. Thats it!!!! It will mark that person as a marker on google maps. The Donate option shows the list of needy people near you and you can go near them and donate.
